Yaqshid District

Location: Mogadishu, Banaadir Region, Somalia

Overview

Yaqshid District (Somali: Degmada Yaaqshiid) is one of the largest and most significant districts in southeastern Mogadishu. It is part of the Banaadir administrative region and includes several bustling neighborhoods.

Neighborhoods

Some of the major neighborhoods in Yaqshid include:

  • Suuq Bacaad
  • Juungal
  • Carafaad
  • Towfiiq
  • Siinaay
  • Fagax
  • Mahad Alla
  • Suuqa Xoolaha
  • Gubta
  • Tawakal
  • Jaamacada Muqdisho (nearby)

Community and Development

The district is largely inhabited by members of the Abgaal subclan of the Hawiye clan. In recent years, Yaqshid has seen improvements in infrastructure and services through various development programs.

One notable project includes a new road connecting General Daud Road to Suuq Bacaad, enhancing transportation within the district. Community policing efforts have also been introduced to strengthen public safety and cooperation between residents and law enforcement.

Landmarks

Important landmarks in or near Yaqshid include:

  • Mogadishu Stadium
  • Villa Somalia (Presidential Palace)
