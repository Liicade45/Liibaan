About Me

Hi, my name is Liibaan Daahir, also known by my nickname Liidaahir. I was born in 2003 in Yaqshiid, a district in Mogadishu, Somalia, and currently live in Towfiiq, another vibrant neighborhood in the city.

I'm a student at Somali National University, where I continue to grow both academically and personally. I have a strong passion for self-learning, and I'm always looking for new ways to improve myself and expand my knowledge.

People often describe me as a social butterfly — I enjoy meeting new people, sharing ideas, and creating meaningful connections. In my free time, I love traveling, reading books, and swimming. These hobbies help me stay balanced, inspired, and energized.

Whether it’s through books, new experiences, or great conversations, I'm always on a journey to learn more and become the best version of myself.
